# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 26, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Re-Leaf** ("we", "our", "us", or "the App"). Re-Leaf is a mobile application that helps users on their journey to quit smoking. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data.

By using Re-Leaf, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

**Data Controller:**
- **App Name:** Re-Leaf
- **Email:** releaf.support@gmail.com
- **Jurisdiction:** Eskişehir, Türkiye

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Name (display name)
- Email address
- Password (stored as encrypted hash by Firebase)
- Authentication provider data (if using Google Sign-In)

### 2.2 App Usage Data
To provide the App's core features, we collect:
- Cigarette consumption habits (cigarettes per day, price per pack)
- Quit date and time
- Reasons for quitting
- Slip-up records (when and notes)
- Wishlist items (goals)
- App preferences (theme, language, notification settings)

### 2.3 Technical Data
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App version
- Approximate location (timezone) — for accurate notification scheduling
- Crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)

### 2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Precise GPS location
- Contacts
- Photos / camera / microphone
- Biometric data
- Browsing history outside the App

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide the App's core functionality (progress tracking, statistics, notifications)
- Authenticate your account
- Send motivational notifications
- Send email verification and password reset
- Improve the App through anonymized analytics
- Detect and fix crashes (Crashlytics)
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **NOT** use your information for:
- Targeted advertising
- Selling to third parties
- Profiling beyond app functionality

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:
- **Contract:** To deliver services you signed up for
- **Consent:** For optional features like notifications
- **Legitimate Interest:** For crash analytics and security
- **Legal Obligation:** When required by law

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your data with the following service providers:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Firebase Authentication (Google) | Account login | Email, name, encrypted password |
| Firebase Firestore (Google) | Data storage | All app data |
| Firebase Crashlytics (Google) | Crash reports | Crash logs, device info |
| Google Sign-In | Optional login | Google account info |
| RevenueCat | Premium purchases | Purchase tokens (no personal data) |

These services have their own privacy policies:
- [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- [Google Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [RevenueCat Privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

We do **NOT** sell or rent your personal data.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on Google Firebase servers (EU/US regions).
- All connections use TLS encryption.
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms.
- Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

We retain your data:
- While your account is active
- Up to 30 days after account deletion (for backup recovery)
- As required by law

## 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, KVKK, and other applicable laws, you have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** Delete your account and data
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Objection:** Object to certain processing
- **Withdraw Consent:** At any time

To exercise these rights:
1. **In-app:** Profile → Delete Account (for full deletion)
2. **Email:** releaf.support@gmail.com

We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Re-Leaf is intended for users **18 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own (e.g., USA, EU). We ensure appropriate safeguards through standard contractual clauses.
